ABOUT US

Home to a 46-bay, multi-tier, floodlit driving range, powered by Toptracer Range technology. Complimented by a practice green and bunker, coffee shop and American Golf Store. There truly
is something for everyone as we also boast two outdoor 18-hole dinosaur themed crazy golf courses.

Please note: we are a cashless venue. The range closes at 10pm with last balls at 9pm.
